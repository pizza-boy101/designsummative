Metaverse

The metaverse in simpler form is a VR world that people can 'live in' such as a realistic 'escape' from this reality.

It's a hybrid of technology features such as virtual reality, augmented reality, and video in which users "live" in a digital realm.
The metaverse's supporters envisage its users working, playing, and remaining connected with pals through everything from concerts and conferences to virtual excursions around the world.
